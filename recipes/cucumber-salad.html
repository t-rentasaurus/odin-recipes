Cucumber salad

Description

Featuring fresh dill, onions, and a sweet, tangy dressing, this cucumber salad recipe is a refreshing summer side dish. Bonus: it couldn't be easier!

Ingredients

  • 2 english cucumbers, very thinly sliced
  • 2 slices of Ham off the bone
  • 2 tbs Smoky mayo (Molly Baz Recipe)
  • 1-2 slices provolone
  • 1/4 Granny smith apple
  • Honey
  • 1/2 Cup arugula
  • Lemon wedge
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, toss together the cucumber, onion, vinegar, honey, and salt. Chill for 20 minutes.
  2. Transfer to a serving bowl, leaving any excess water behind. Sprinkle with the dill and chives, if desired. Season with several grinds of pepper and serve.
