Best Egg Salad Sammy

Description

The BEST egg salad recipe! Delicious on a sandwich or straight from the bowl, this easy egg salad is creamy, tangy, and bright. A perfect lunch!

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 to 3 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup finely diced red onion
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
  • 1 teaspoon capers, optional

Steps

  1. Hard boil the eggs
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, mustard, lemon juice, salt, and several grinds of pepper.
  3. Mix in the onions, then fold in the eggs, chives, dill, and capers, if using.
  4. Cover and chill in the refrigerator for 20 minutes. Stir, then season to taste and serve.
